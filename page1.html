About Us

Approx. 30-40% of food is wasted in India every day. And there are a lot of people who are sleeping with empty stomachs, as they are not in a position to afford food. So, we came up with this solution that the food which is cooked but unserved can be donated to the people in need, instead of throwing them in the dustbins. So, for this to happen, we are gonna tie up with different NGOs. And if at all the food is not reached to the people in need, then the food can be used to generate electricity by converting the food into biogas. As from the food which is rotted, Methane is released into the environment which is not safe. So, by generating electricity from that food reduces the greenhouse emissions as well. By this solution, we can manage the food waste very effectively, which benefits both the needy people and the environment
